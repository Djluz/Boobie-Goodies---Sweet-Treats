Product Details:

Our White Choc cookies are a customer favourite, filled with delicious flavour in every bite.

Specially formulated for breastfeeding Mums. Packed with nutritious ingredients our Lactation Cookies make the ideal snack to have packed in your hospital bag, next to your feeding chair or on hand throughout your busy days. Our White Choc cookies were the original flavour of the bunch and are still one of our top sellers.

Packs of 12 delicious pre-baked goodness.

Ingredients:

Wheat Flour, Oats, Butter (cream (from milk), salt), Brown Sugar, Sugar, White Chocolate Compound (10%) [sugar, vegetable fat ( emulsifiers (492, soy lecithin)), whole milk powder, emulsifier (soy lecithin), flavour], Egg, Water, Brewer Yeast, Linseed, Raising Agent (450, 500), Flavour.

Contains: Gluten, Egg, Milk & Soy
May contain: Peanuts & Tree Nuts
