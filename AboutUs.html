ABOUT US


100% PH OWNED & OPERATED

Renee Ball, the founder of BGST, was born to help others. She began her career as a childcare provider, primarily with newborns. Renee then began studies in Midwifery before becoming a mother herself. Some would say Renee was destined to be in the baking industry. Her father was one of the countrys top bakers, and she worked in the family bakeries from an early age. Although a career in baking was never planned, Renee began making lactation cookies for herself while at home with her baby daughter Hunter. With the support of her husband Tim, this is where the journey started. As requests from fellow moms grew and local markets beckoned, Renee turned a home-baking hobby into a full-time job. As a Mom Renee understands the needs of the worlds most important job, this is what drives her to succeed. Renee has also discovered a love for business and continuously strives to learn to run and grow BGST efficiently while juggling her life as a mother of 2, Hunter & Harley.


WE AIM TO PROVIDE NUTRITIONAL AND FUNCTIONAL PRODUCTS TO BREASTFEEDING MOTHERS.

BGST provides affordable, functional & great tasting products that support breastfeeding Mothers.

From consumable lactation goods filled with galactagogues, to beautifully designed breastfeeding bras. BGST began by producing (the now famous) lactation cookies.

Using known galactagogues filled ingredients that support breastfeeding mothers. Breastfeeding mothers can burn up to an extra 500 calories a day compared to a non- breastfeeding Mom. This means that their bodies are working overtime. It is vital that breastfeeding mothers replace these calories with the right nutrient dense foods to ensure optimal health not only for themselves but for their babies too.

Historically, functional lactation treats for new or expectant mothers in PH have been hard to find or homemade. BGSTn has filled this gap by providing a good selection of cookies, blends, and tea and continuously innovating new products to introduce to the market. BGST has formulated recipes using specific ingredients that contain galactagogues.

These galactagogues have been a long-touted substance to help humans increase their milk supply. Taste is the key. When a mothers main concern is her baby, BGST provides top quality products that can be enjoyed by Mom (and Dad if he feels the need). If the mother is satisfied, this will help her to relax while breastfeeding which supports the let-down effect, (the let-down effect means your milk is ready to flow).
